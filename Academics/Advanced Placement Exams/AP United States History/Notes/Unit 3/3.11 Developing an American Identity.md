* Creating an American identity involved doing away with many British customs
* State constitutions did away with titles of nobility and [primogeniture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primogeniture)
	* Heightened by the confiscation of Loyalist estates
* Most states agreed to separation of church and state
	* Means not giving financial support to any religious group
	* The Anglican Church was disestablished in the south
* Only NH, CT, and MA gave state support to the Congregational Church (until 1830s)
* Development of political parties added to + reflected the American identity
* Distinctions between F's and D-R's had origins over the ratification of the Constitution ([[3.10 Shaping a New Republic#First Political Parties|see 3.10]])
